# Sample QAs for Chatbot

## INTRODUCTION AND VALUES

**Q:**  
What is the purpose of the company policy document

**Expected answer:**  
To support people, protect dignity, and create a safe, fair, and positive workplace.

**Q:**  
Do company policies apply equally to owners and leadership

**Expected answer:**  
Yes, the policies apply equally to everyone, including the owner and leadership team.

**Q:**  
What behavior is considered unacceptable at the workplace

**Expected answer:**  
Shouting, insulting language, threats, bullying, or humiliation.

**Q:**

**Expected answer:**  
Mistakes are considered part of learning, but hiding mistakes is not acceptable.

## WORKING HOURS

**Q:**  
What are the normal working hours as per company policy

**Expected answer:**  
Up to 9 hours a day and 48 hours a week as per Gujarat Shops and Establishments Act.

**Q:**  
Is work from home allowed according to company policy

**Expected answer:**  
Yes, where work allows, flexible hours or work from home may be provided based on role and performance.

---

## LEAVE POLICY

**Q:**  
What types of leave are provided by the company

**Expected answer:**  
Earned leave, casual leave, sick leave, national and festival holidays, maternity leave, and caregiver leave where possible.

**Q:**  
Does the company require medical certificates for short illness

**Expected answer:**  
No, medical certificates may be requested only for extended absences.

---

## MATERNITY AND POSH

**Q:**  
Which law governs maternity benefits in the company

**Expected answer:**  
Maternity Benefit Act, 1961.

**Q:**  
How does the company handle sexual harassment complaints

**Expected answer:**  
Through an Internal Complaints Committee as per the POSH Act, 2013, with confidentiality and no retaliation.

---

## GRIEVANCE AND DISCIPLINE

**Q:**  
What is the grievance redressal process in the company

**Expected answer:**  
Employees first speak to their supervisor, then escalate to management or HR, and formal review if needed.

**Q:**  
Is disciplinary action taken without listening to employees

**Expected answer:**  
No, disciplinary action is taken only after fair inquiry and explanation opportunity.

---

## DATA AND CONFIDENTIALITY

**Q:**  
How is employee personal data handled

**Expected answer:**  
Employee data is kept confidential and used only for lawful purposes.

---

## LEGAL COMPLIANCE

**Q:**

**Expected answer:**  
Gujarat Shops and Establishments Act, Minimum Wages Act, Payment of Wages Act, EPF Act, ESI Act, POSH Act, and others listed.
