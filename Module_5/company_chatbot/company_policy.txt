1. INTRODUCTION AND PURPOSE

This policy document explains how our company works and how we treat each other. It exists to create clarity, fairness, and trust. It is not written to control people. It is written to support people.

Every organisation needs shared rules so that everyone knows what is right, what is expected, and what support is available. Clear policies reduce confusion. Fair policies reduce fear. Human policies build long-term relationships.

Our company believes that work should not damage dignity, health, or personal life. Work should provide income, learning, and stability. People should feel safe, respected, and heard.

This document reflects our core values. These values are honesty, respect, responsibility, learning, and fairness. These values apply equally to owners, managers, employees, interns, trainees, and contract staff.

Policies are meaningful only when they are followed by everyone. No one in the company has special privilege to ignore these rules. Authority does not remove responsibility. Seniority does not remove accountability.

This document is written in simple language so that everyone can understand it easily.

2. SCOPE AND APPLICABILITY

These policies apply to every person working with the company in any form. This includes owners, partners, directors, managers, supervisors, employees, interns, trainees, apprentices, consultants, and contractual workers.

There are no separate rules based on designation, salary, gender, age, or employment type.

The policies apply during working hours, at the workplace, during official travel, during company events, and during any activity connected with company work.

All people are expected to follow these policies with sincerity. At the same time, the company commits to applying these policies fairly, patiently, and consistently.

3. EMPLOYMENT TERMS AND CODE OF CONDUCT

Employment in the company is based on mutual respect and trust. The company offers fair opportunity and safe working conditions. In return, every person is expected to work honestly and responsibly.

Everyone must perform their duties to the best of their ability. Work should be done carefully and on time. If someone does not know something, asking for help is encouraged.

Honesty is a core value. False reporting, hiding mistakes, or misleading others is not acceptable. Mistakes should be reported early so they can be corrected.

Professional behaviour is expected at all times. This includes polite language, calm communication, and respectful conduct.

Abusive language, threats, shouting, or humiliation of any person is not allowed, even during stress or disagreement.

Everyone must respect confidentiality related to company work, clients, and colleagues.

4. WORKING HOURS, ATTENDANCE, AND FLEXIBILITY

The company follows working hours as per Gujarat Shops and Establishments rules. Standard working hours will be informed clearly.

Regular attendance and punctuality are important for teamwork. Everyone should make sincere effort to be on time.

If someone is late or absent, they should inform their reporting person as early as possible. Sudden emergencies are understood.

The company understands that people have families, health needs, and personal responsibilities. Reasonable flexibility may be provided where work allows.

Overtime work is not encouraged as a habit. It may be required only during urgent business needs. Overtime will be handled fairly and as per law.

No one is expected to work continuously without rest. Breaks are necessary for safety and productivity.

5. LEAVE POLICY

The company believes that rest is essential for physical and mental health. Taking leave is a normal part of work life.

Paid Leave
Eligible workers will receive paid leave as per applicable law and company norms. Paid leave can be used for rest, personal work, or family needs.

Sick Leave
Sick leave is provided for illness or medical needs. No one should feel pressured to work when unwell. Health information will be treated with respect and privacy.

Festival and National Holidays
National and state holidays will be observed as required. Festival holidays will be communicated in advance.

Maternity Leave
Maternity leave will be provided as per Indian law. The company supports the health, safety, and dignity of women employees.

Paternity Leave
Paternity leave is provided to support family bonding and shared responsibility.

Unpaid Leave
Unpaid leave may be taken when paid leave is exhausted, after discussion and approval.

Leave approval will be reasonable and transparent. Leave will not be denied without valid reason.

6. SALARY, WAGES, INCENTIVES, AND PAYMENTS

Salary and wages will always meet or exceed minimum wage requirements.

Payments will be made on time. Delayed payment damages trust and is not acceptable.

Salary structure will be explained clearly. Deductions, if any, will be lawful and transparent.

Incentives or bonuses, if offered, will be based on clear and fair criteria such as performance or company results.

Payslips or payment details will be shared whenever applicable.

The company respects the financial dignity of every worker.

7. WORKPLACE BEHAVIOUR, RESPECT, AND EQUALITY

The workplace must be safe, inclusive, and respectful for everyone.

Discrimination based on gender, caste, religion, age, disability, marital status, language, or background is strictly not allowed.

Everyone has the right to be treated with dignity. Personal jokes, comments, or behaviour that hurt someoneâ€™s feelings are not acceptable.

Differences of opinion should be handled calmly and professionally.

Discipline means self-control and responsibility. It does not mean fear or punishment.

8. POSH AND ANTI-HARASSMENT POLICY

The company has zero tolerance for sexual harassment.

Sexual harassment includes any unwanted physical contact, verbal comments, gestures, messages, or behaviour that makes a person uncomfortable.

Every person has the right to feel safe at work.

Complaints will be handled confidentially, respectfully, and without bias.

No one will face punishment or pressure for raising a genuine complaint.

False complaints made with harmful intention will also be reviewed fairly.

9. HEALTH, SAFETY, AND WORKPLACE HYGIENE

The company is committed to a clean and safe working environment.

Basic safety rules must be followed by everyone, without exception.

Safety equipment will be provided where required. It must be used properly.

Any unsafe condition or accident must be reported immediately.

The workplace must be kept clean. Everyone shares responsibility for hygiene.

Health emergencies will be handled with urgency and care.

10. PERFORMANCE, LEARNING, AND CAREER GROWTH

The company believes people grow when they learn. Learning is encouraged at all levels.

Performance feedback will be honest, respectful, and focused on improvement.

Training may be provided based on job role and future needs.

Growth opportunities will be based on skills, effort, behaviour, and consistency.

Everyone will be given a fair chance to improve before any negative decision.

11. USE OF COMPANY PROPERTY AND INFORMATION

Company property includes equipment, documents, systems, and data.

These must be used responsibly and only for work purposes.

Company information is confidential and must not be shared without permission.

Data misuse or leakage is a serious concern and will be handled carefully.

Limited personal use of company resources may be allowed if it does not affect work.

12. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL AND OPEN COMMUNICATION

The company encourages open and honest communication.

Any concern, complaint, or suggestion can be shared without fear.

Issues will be listened to patiently and handled fairly.

Confidentiality will be maintained as much as possible.

No retaliation or unfair treatment is allowed against anyone who raises a concern.

13. DISCIPLINARY PROCESS AND FAIRNESS

Disciplinary action is taken only when necessary.

The purpose is correction, not punishment.

Every person will get a fair chance to explain their side.

Decisions will be unbiased and based on facts.

The same standards apply to everyone, including management.

14.RESIGNATION, NOTICE PERIOD, AND EXIT

Resignation should be given in writing with the required notice period.

Notice period will be the same for everyone at the same level.

The company will support smooth handover of work.

Final settlement will be completed on time.

Experience and relieving letters will be issued fairly and without delay.

15. POLICY REVIEW, UPDATES, AND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

Policies may be reviewed to improve fairness and clarity.

Any changes will be shared clearly with everyone.

Suggestions for improvement are always welcome.

Every person is expected to understand and follow these policies.

This policy document reflects our commitment to dignity, trust, fairness, and shared growth.
The company grows when its people feel safe, respected, and valued.
